# The 15-Minute Weekly Review

To keep your technical second brain and career trajectory sharp, execute this checklist every Friday afternoon before logging off. It takes less than 15 minutes.

## 1. The Brain Dump (5 mins)
Did I solve a complex bug or encounter a bizarre error this week?
- [ ] Yes: Add the error and the exact CLI command/fix to the relevant file in `99-cheatsheets/`.
- [ ] No: Skip.

## 2. The Project Log (5 mins)
Did I complete a major epic, project, or feature this week?
- [ ] Yes: Open `project-tracker.md` and write a single STAR-method bullet point. (Do this now while the metrics, like "reduced TAT by 2 seconds", are fresh in your memory).
- [ ] No: Skip.

## 3. The Skills Check (5 mins)
Look at your `career-dashboard.md` and `certification-tracker.md`.
- [ ] Did I make progress on my current target skill (e.g., AWS/Docker)?
- [ ] If I have an interview next week, which 2 files from `01-interview-prep/` will I review?

*Rule: If you do nothing else, just update the Project Tracker. Do not wait until you are looking for a job to try and remember what you accomplished 8 months ago.*
