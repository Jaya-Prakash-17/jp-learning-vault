### Certification Philosophy
- Do not collect certifications like trading cards.
- Only pursue a certification if it directly closes a gap identified in your `skill-gap-analysis.md` (e.g., AWS closes the Cloud gap).
- Stop at the Associate level for cloud certs unless you are explicitly moving into DevOps. (A Solution Architect needs to know AWS exists and how to design with it, but doesn't necessarily need the deep networking knowledge of the Professional tier).
