# Career Transition (AI to EDI)

**Question**: Your degree is in AI & Data Science. Why are you working in Healthcare EDI?

**How to Frame It**: It is not a pivot *away* from data; it is a pivot into the *plumbing* of data.

**Key Points**:
- **Data Foundation**: AI and analytics rely entirely on the availability, cleanliness, and security of data.
- **The Reality of Healthcare**: I realized that before you can apply advanced AI to healthcare, you have to solve the interoperability problem. The data is often locked in complex X12 formats and legacy systems.
- **Strategic Value**: By mastering EDI, HL7, FHIR, and integration middleware (Edifecs), I am learning how enterprise healthcare data actually moves and transforms in the real world.
- **The Long-Term Vision**: Combining my AI background with deep knowledge of healthcare data pipelines positions me to eventually build intelligent, agentic systems that operate directly on complex healthcare workflows.
