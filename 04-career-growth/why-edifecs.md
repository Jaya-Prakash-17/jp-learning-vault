# Why Edifecs?

**Question**: Why Edifecs? / Why did you join Edifecs?

**Key Points**:
- **Industry Standard**: Edifecs is the gold standard for healthcare interoperability and EDI processing in the US market.
- **Platform Breadth**: Working here allows me hands-on experience with the entire data lifecycle—from mapping in MapBuilder and SpecBuilder, to orchestrating with Route, DataFlow, and Native Translator.
- **Client Exposure**: It gives me direct access to massive payer environments (like Humana and the State of Colorado), providing real-world context to how healthcare data flows nationally.
- **Technical Growth**: It bridges traditional EDI with modern API orchestration (SOAP/REST, JSON/XML transformation), providing a strong foundation in both legacy systems and modern integration patterns.
