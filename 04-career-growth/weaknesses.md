# Weaknesses

**Question**: What is your greatest weakness?

**Weakness: Over-committing to manual optimization / Hesitance to delegate early**
- *Context*: Because I have a strong technical background and like to understand systems end-to-end (from the element-level data mapping down to the Groovy pipeline scripts), I sometimes spend too much time trying to manually optimize a specific component myself instead of stepping back or leveraging standard out-of-the-box features.
- *Mitigation*: I am actively learning to rely more on architectural best practices and team collaboration. For example, during the Humana project, instead of attempting to isolate pipeline bottlenecks alone, I collaborated with senior engineers on Native Translator tuning, which solved the issue much faster.
