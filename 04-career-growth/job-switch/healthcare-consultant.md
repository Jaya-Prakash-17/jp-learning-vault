# Role Profile: Healthcare Consultant / Edifecs Consultant

## The Role
This is the direct evolution of your current position. Senior Implementation Consultants act as the bridge between the client's business needs and the technical reality of the middleware. You are the EDI SME (Subject Matter Expert).

## Core Responsibilities
- Gathering requirements from Payers/Providers (e.g., "We need to map this proprietary DB format to an 834").
- Leading the configuration of SpecBuilder profiles and MapBuilder logic.
- Executing UAT (User Acceptance Testing) with external trading partners.
- Triaging production issues (e.g., finding out why a massive 837 batch failed SNIP 2 validation).

## Why You Want This
- **Low Risk, High Reward**: You already know how to do this. You have the Humana and Colorado experience. Moving to a Senior Consultant role at a competitor (or promoting internally) comes with a significant pay bump for leveraging your existing Edifecs/X12 knowledge.
- **Client Exposure**: This role develops your soft skills and client-facing communication, which is required for eventually becoming a Solution Architect.

## The Interview Focus
To pass an interview for this role, you must demonstrate:
1. **Domain Mastery**: You must be able to rattle off X12 segments from memory and explain CAQH CORE operating rules.
2. **Client Management**: The interviewer will ask behavioral questions about handling difficult clients or pushing back against unrealistic go-live deadlines.
3. **End-to-End Troubleshooting**: They will give you a scenario (e.g., "A provider says they sent a file, but we don't see it"). You must explain exactly how you trace it from the SFTP drop, through the TA1, into TM, and down to the specific failed route.

## Target Keywords for Resume
- EDI X12 (837, 835, 27X, 834)
- Edifecs SpecBuilder / MapBuilder
- Trading Partner Onboarding
- SNIP Validation
- UAT Testing
