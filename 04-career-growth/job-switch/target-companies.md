# Target Companies & Sectors

When looking to switch jobs, do not apply blindly. The healthcare IT sector is vast, but it is divided into distinct categories. Your experience at Edifecs (working with Humana and Colorado) makes you highly valuable to specific players.

## 1. The Payers (Insurance Companies)
*Why target them: They have massive IT budgets, great benefits, and usually own Edifecs or similar middleware.*
- **Top Targets**: UnitedHealth Group (Optum), Humana, CVS Health (Aetna), Centene, Elevance Health (Anthem), various Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS) state organizations.
- **What they want**: Engineers who understand CAQH CORE, 27X real-time routing, and how to protect their internal Facets/QNXT adjudication engines from bad provider data.

## 2. The Clearinghouses & Network Hubs
*Why target them: They process the highest volume of EDI in the world. Excellent place for hardcore Integration Engineers.*
- **Top Targets**: Change Healthcare (Optum), Zelis, Availity, Waystar, Clearsense.
- **What they want**: Engineers obsessed with high-throughput, low-latency parsing. Deep X12 SNIP rule knowledge and ability to map complex custom formats to standard EDI.

## 3. The Technology Vendors (Direct Competitors to Edifecs)
*Why target them: You know exactly how their software works because you worked for their direct rival.*
- **Top Targets**: Cognizant (TriZetto), Gainwell Technologies, Epic Systems, Cerner (Oracle), InterSystems (HealthShare), MuleSoft (Salesforce - Healthcare division).
- **What they want**: Implementation consultants who can deploy their software at client sites and guide non-technical hospital IT staff through Go-Lives.

## 4. The Healthcare Tech Consultancies
*Why target them: Incredible exposure to modern cloud architectures. You will travel (or work remotely) across various clients.*
- **Top Targets**: Slalom, Deloitte, Accenture, Nordic Consulting.
- **What they want**: Agile, fast-learning consultants with deep domain knowledge who can communicate effectively with C-suite executives and lead digital transformation projects.

### Strategy Note
Because you already worked internally at **Edifecs**, every company that *purchases* Edifecs software (which includes Humana, BCBS, and many state Medicaid agencies like Colorado) will view your resume as top-tier. You are the "insider" who knows how the engine works under the hood. Leverage this heavily.
