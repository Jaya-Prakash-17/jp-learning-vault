# Career Story

**Question**: Walk me through your career journey.

**The Arc**:
- **Education**: B.Tech in AI & Data Science (2021-2025). Gained a strong analytical foundation and published research papers on deep learning (lung cancer, brain tumor classification).
- **Initial Exposure**: Data Analyst Intern at YoShops (2023). Got hands-on experience with Power BI, Advanced Excel, and data transformation at scale.
- **The Pivot**: Transitioned into Healthcare EDI, recognizing the massive scale and critical real-world impact of healthcare interoperability.
- **Current Role**: Associate Implementation Consultant at Edifecs (Cotiviti). 
- **Key Win**: Played a major role in Humana’s real-time 276/277 modernization, contributing to an 80% reduction in turnaround time for hundreds of thousands of daily transactions.

**Why it matters**: Demonstrates a progression from raw data science and analytics into high-scale, mission-critical healthcare integrations.
