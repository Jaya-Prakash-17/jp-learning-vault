# Why Healthcare?

**Question**: Why did you choose to build your career in healthcare IT?

**Key Points**:
- **High Impact**: Healthcare data interoperability directly impacts patient care and provider efficiency. Reducing a claim status turnaround from 13.5s to 2.5s isn't just an IT win; it means faster answers for patients and providers at the point of care.
- **Scale and Complexity**: The sheer volume (handling 200K+ daily transactions) and strict regulatory requirements (HIPAA, CAQH CORE) offer complex engineering challenges that I enjoy solving.
- **Continuous Evolution**: The shift from traditional X12 EDI towards real-time APIs, HL7, and FHIR means the industry is undergoing a massive technical modernization phase, which is an exciting time to be an integration engineer.
- **Personal Background**: My academic research focused on applying AI to medical imaging (lung cancer, brain tumors), so I have always been driven by the intersection of technology and healthcare outcomes.
